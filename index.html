02 / Design philosophy

Calm under
stage pressure.

01

Local first

The show should not depend on a distant service. Performance data stays close, responsive, and under the band’s control.

02

Clear boundaries

Shared playback, the upcoming queue, and each performer’s private preparation remain distinct by design.

03

Performance focused

Large targets, high contrast, and deliberate information hierarchy help musicians act quickly in low-light environments.

04

Band shaped

Songs, members, libraries, and setlists reflect familiar band workflows instead of forcing the stage into office software.

05 / Current status

Active development

A working prototype,
still finding its final form.

LiveStage Pro currently includes a native macOS host, an embedded local server, and a browser-based performer client. Core show-state and content workflows are being built and refined.

The project is not yet available as a public release. This site will track meaningful progress as development continues.
